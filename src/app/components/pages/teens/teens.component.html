Teen Instruction - 17 & Under

Behind The Wheel

This course is required by the DMV for teenages in order to obtain their license. It consist of 7 sessions in the car. Each session will be 50 minutes driving and 50 minutes observing. The final test is included. In each session the student will have the opportunity to improve his/her driving skills. Upon successful completion of the course, Potomac Driving School will issue a temporary license valid for 6 months. This document along with the Learner’s Permit will be the license for the new driver. The student will be exempt from the road skills test at the DMV. Within the next few months the juvenile court will send a letter with the date and time of the Licensing Ceremony. At the Ceremony the student will receive the hard copy of the license. Requirements: •Driver Education Certificate issued by the High School or other DMV approved school •Photocopy of Learner’s permit •Payment
