Driving your car comes with a lot of responsibility. Having the most knowledge and skills in driving will help you become the best and safest driver you can be. Potomac Driving School will help you get to that level. Our instructors are trained and licensed by the Virginia Department of Motor Vehicles, including an annual FBI background check. We are full licensed, insured and bonded for your protection.

Our belief is to form safe, responsible, cautious and defensive drivers. Focusing on the specific skills necessary in helping you prevent and avoid collisions is one of our main goals. Our rules and regulations are based on the Virginia Department of Motor Vehicles guidelines. Our vehicles are equipped with dual brake control, allowing you and the instructor to be as safe as possible.

We are also bilingual. By speaking multiple languages, we can ensure we are able to help as many people as possible. Whether you speak English or Spanish, you’ll be able to enroll in our driving services.
